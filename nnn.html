Titration Indicators & Structures

4. Why are two different indicators used in titration?

One indicator is used to detect carbonate, and another is used to detect bicarbonate, ensuring distinct endpoints for each species in the carbonate system. [web:18]

5. Why can water alkalinity not be due to simultaneous presence of OH⁻, CO₃²⁻ and HCO₃⁻?

OH⁻ and HCO₃⁻ react to form CO₃²⁻ and water, so all three cannot persist together at equilibrium in measurable amounts during titration. [web:18]
